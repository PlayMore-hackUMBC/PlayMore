About the Project

The idea behind this website was to produce a site where users of differing abilities could rate video games based on their accessability features.

By splitting reviws into different categories user will be able to quickly identify id a game would be accomedating to their needs.

Tech Specs

PlayMore was developed in under 24 hours for hackUMBC 2023. We utilized several frameworks such as Angular and Firestore and apis such as RAWG.
